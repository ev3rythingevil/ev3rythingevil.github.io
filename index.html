Project: Band Together

Band Together is an application created to help musicians find other local players in order to form a band. Upon signing up, members can click through profiles that appear on their main page, and chat with mutual matches. The app also uses location information to only show you users within a certain radius.

In this application, random tarot cards were generated for users, and displayed with a brief description of the card and it's meaning. Users could save cards to a database, so they could look back at the history of their readings.

Project: Monster Compendium

Using Vanilla JavaScript, HTML and CSS, my first project focused on creating an interactive single page application. Pulling from a mock API, a list of Dungeons and Dragons monsters is rendered with important information. Users can also adjust things such as a monster's total hit points, roll dice, and add their own creations to the library.
